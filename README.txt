This is a template for producing Ph.D. dissertations and masters theses conforming to the documents "Dissertation Filing Guide" and "Thesis Filing Guide" produced by the UC Berkeley Graduate Division and available on the web at <http://grad.berkeley.edu/policies/>.  The template is currently based on the dissertation and thesis guides, dated May 2010 -- December 2013 and February 2011 -- February 2014, respectively.
